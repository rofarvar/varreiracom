About globalized food

At Globalized, we have always felt our mission was to serve all, and we have decided that the best way to do that was to globalize our menu to all types of food and make it avaiable to as many people as possible, hence the food truck. And, luckily, all of the founders are from different places, even strengthening the idea of an international food truck.

Globalized has something for everybody, from our signature tacos and salmon as entrees, to our best dessert, chocolate cheesecake.
